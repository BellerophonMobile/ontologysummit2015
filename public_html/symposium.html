Ontology Summit 2015

2015 Ontology Summit Symposium

Following its established pattern, this year's Ontology Summit consists of a series of weekly virtual discussions, followed by a 2-day face-to-face symposium.

Schedule

This year's face-to-face symposium will be April 13 and 14. Several excellent keynote speakers, expert panels, and group discussions are on the docket for this year.See the Schedule...

Venue

We will again be graciously hosted by NITRD at the NSF in Arlington, Virginia, USA:

  • NSF Boardroom (room 1235)
    Stafford Place I
    4201 Wilson Boulevard
    Arlington, VA 22230, USA

A map of the site and directions from the nearby Ballston-MU Metro station are available here.

Registration

The symposium is free of charge, but all physical attendees must register in advance. Register!
